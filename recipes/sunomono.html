Sunomono

Description

Japanese sunomono is delicious and simple to make by marinating cucumbers in a sweet and sour vinaigrette for a refreshing salad or side dish

Ingredients

  • 2 large cucumbers, peeled
  • 1/3 cup rice vinegar
  • 4 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Cut cucumbers in half lengthwise and scoop out any large seeds. Slice crosswise into very thin slices.
  2. Place vinegar, sugar, ginger, and salt in a small bowl; mix well to combine. Add cucumber slices and stir to coat. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving.
